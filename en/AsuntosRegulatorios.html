Regulatory Affairs

Over the years, the firm has developed a highly technical and specialized area related to the health regulation of food, beverages, cosmetics, and household cleaning products; complementing the professional practice of intellectual property, trademarks, and patents initiated in 1946. We currently represent and advise the most important companies in the food industry and retail sector. This practice area offers the following services:

  • Registration in R.N.E., R.N.P.A., municipal and provincial registries
  • Procedures with S.E.N.A.S.A. for food and animal products
  • Registration of Personal Hygiene, Cosmetic, and Household Cleaning Products
  • Product, label, and packaging audits
  • Technical and legal advisory
  • Defense against health-related sanctions
  • Processing and advisory for domestic and imported products before national and local authorities
  • Label design and validation. Free circulation certificates
  • Registration of food contact materials and utensils
  • Comprehensive consulting and defense against Food Code violations

  • Advisory on warranties, corporate responsibility, and consumer rights
  • Assistance with consumer complaints and government entities
  • Legal defense against administrative sanctions
  • Representation in Arbitration Courts
