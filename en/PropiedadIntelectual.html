Intellectual Property

Intellectual property is global but protection is territorial. We advise you on designing a comprehensive protection strategy and making the right decisions at the right time. For over 30 years, we have helped companies identify, protect, defend, and add value to their intellectual property assets both locally and abroad.

Our litigation practice allows us to assess contingencies realistically, and anticipate or prevent conflicts. We work to minimize risks, avoid unnecessary costs, and optimize results.

Trademarks and Trade Names

  • Searches of registry and usage background. Assessment of registrability and availability.
  • Registration procedure in Argentina and worldwide.
  • Monitoring and follow-up until the title is obtained.
  • WATCHING SERVICE.
  • Opposition defense.
  • License, franchise, coexistence, and transfer agreements.
  • Registration of transfers.
  • Legal audits (Due Diligence).
  • Legal actions and claims for infringements.

Patents, Utility Models, and Industrial Designs
