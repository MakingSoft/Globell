History

Since 1946, commitment to our clients.

Firm Profile

Established in Argentina in 1946.

The firm was founded by Attorneys Ricardo Gordó Fornés and Ricardo Gordó Sola, continuing its trajectory with Ricardo V. Gordó Llobell and Rosa Llobell Ahuir de Gordó Sola.

Currently, following the generational path, the fourth generation composed of Attorneys Ricardo M. Gordó Llobell (son) and Maria Gabriela Gordó Llobell participate in the firm's leadership.

Industrial and Intellectual Property and specific topics of Health Regulation have been the main focus of the firm since its beginnings and remain its specialties.

Throughout the years, the firm has maintained the same characteristic inherited from its founders: commitment to clients.

Gordó Llobell & Associates preserves this commitment by providing personalized, high-quality service that meets the highest professional standards.

Our Vision

Our experience in trademarks, patents, copyrights and other areas of Intellectual Property (IP) supports entrepreneurs and companies working on building and developing their intangible assets with a future-oriented vision.

Considering ourselves an IP laboratory and examining each matter under a microscope makes us evaluate every situation carefully and analyze even the smallest details.

We are not just another IP firm - that's why we choose to focus on being an IP laboratory with an in-depth perspective on each case.

We apply the same approach to developing our regulatory affairs area, transforming administrative processing into strategic consulting.

Our Mission

We work to become our clients' strategic partner, helping them capitalize on all benefits derived from their projects to the furthest extent possible.

We specialize in intellectual property and regulatory affairs consulting, and we will never stop seeking innovative ways to protect and safeguard your intangible assets to make each of our clients more successful.

We will demonstrate that we are the best partner your company can have for your IP and regulatory affairs needs.
